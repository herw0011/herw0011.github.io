How can you book a project space?

Step 1: go to HZ portal.

Step 2: Click on the self service portal.

Step 3: Click on reservate.

Step 4: Choose the correct category and search for the 'resource' you want to reserve.

Step 5: Select the day when you want to borrow.

Step 6: Drag your mouse over the time where you want to make a reservation.

Step 7: Click on next.

Step 8: Read the loan conditions, check the reservation and click next.

Step 9: Fill in the necessary information and click on confirm reservation.
